Ancient Indian Myths and Legends

The Ramayana

The epic tale of Lord Rama, his devoted wife Sita, and his loyal brother Lakshmana. The story highlights the triumph of good over evil.

The Mahabharata

The grand epic of the Kurukshetra war, featuring the Pandavas, the Kauravas, and the wisdom of Lord Krishna.
